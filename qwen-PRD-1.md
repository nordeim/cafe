# ANALYSIS & PLANNING: Singapore Coffee Shop Website PRD

## Executive Summary
I will create a comprehensive Product Requirements Document (PRD) for a Singapore-based coffee shop website that replicates the design language, UX patterns, and visual aesthetics of the websitedemos.net/coffee-shop-04 template while adapting it to local Singaporean context, culture, and business needs.

---

## PHASE 1: DEEP ANALYSIS

### Current Template Analysis (Based on Research)
The websitedemos.net coffee-shop-04 template exhibits several key design characteristics:

**Design Language & Aesthetics:**
- Minimalistic yet highly creative coffee shop website template with modern design elements 
- Combination of contemporary and heritage design that reflects the authentic café atmosphere 
- Smart parallax effect on the homepage creating depth and visual interest 
- Rich imagery, warm color palette, and strategic whitespace for visual balance 
- Well-designed graphics and flexible content areas for storytelling 

**Key UI Components:**
- Hero section with compelling headline ("Tailored-Made Coffee") and descriptive subtext
- Service/product showcase areas for featured drinks and coffee-related products 
- Menu section integration for food and beverage offerings 
- Responsive layout that adapts to all device sizes 
- Modern navigation patterns with clear information architecture

**UX Patterns:**
- Smooth scrolling with parallax effects for immersive storytelling 
- Component-based structure for easy customization and maintenance 
- Clear call-to-action placement for conversion optimization
- Visual hierarchy that guides users through the coffee shop experience

### Singapore Market Adaptation Requirements

**Cultural Adaptations:**
- Incorporate Singapore's multicultural heritage (Chinese, Malay, Indian, Western influences)
- Feature local Singaporean coffee culture elements (Kopi, traditional brewing methods)
- Bilingual content support (English primary, with key phrases in Mandarin/Malay/Tamil)
- Local imagery showcasing Singaporean coffee shop ambiance and community

**Business Model Adaptations:**
- Integration with Singapore food delivery platforms (GrabFood, FoodPanda, Deliveroo)
- Local payment gateways (PayNow, GrabPay, DBS PayLah!)
- Location-specific features for Singapore neighborhoods (CBD, Tiong Bahru, Katong, etc.)
- Compliance with Singapore PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) requirements

**Technical Considerations:**
- Optimized for Singapore internet speeds and mobile-first usage patterns
- SEO optimization for Singapore local search ("best coffee shop Singapore", "kopitiam near me")
- Integration with Singapore business directories and review platforms
- Performance optimization for SEA region hosting
